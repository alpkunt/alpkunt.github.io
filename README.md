## Projects

### 🍽️ The Taste Atlas
An interactive culinary cartography — 40 national cuisines scored across 19 flavor
and ingredient axes, then mapped by how alike they actually *taste*. Includes a
heatmap world map, six data-drawn taste regions, and a signature-dish explorer.
Method and full data are documented inside the app itself.

→ https://alpkunt.github.io/taste-atlas/
